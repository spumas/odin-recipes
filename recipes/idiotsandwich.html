What even is an idiot sandwich?

Okay, what do we need?



- 2 slices of bread


- 1 idiot



Uhhh... Okay. Now what?



Take the two slices of bread, one in each hand.


Locate the nearest idiot.


Walk up to them, hold the 2 slices of bread against their ears. Yell at their face in a loud manner:


WHAT ARE YOU?


The answer should be clear: An idiot sandwich. Voila!
